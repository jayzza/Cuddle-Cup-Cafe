Name's History:
Ever wonder why its named Cuddle Cup Cafe? Here's why. Every word has its meaning. First is cuddle which is synonymous to hug which is an expression of love. Secondly, cup is a medium or container where you enjoy your coffee and lastly, cafe is a place where you can bond and celebrate with friends, family, and love ones. Briefly, Cuddle Cup Dafe is a place where you can feeel love and be at home.

History of Cuddle Cup Cafe:
The owner herself, Lovella Mendoza-Go, has always been fond of black coffee since childhood and her dream of having a shop started on 2007. She proclaimed (TRP Mastery Seminar 2012) and got her inspirations in 2015 on Feast Talks on Discipileships in a Coffe shop and a freind who opened a cafae in the province. Mrs. Go worked towards her dream where she atended expos, seminars, and read everal books. Gladly, opportunity came in 2015 and finally on the 9th of January of 2016, Cuddle Cup Cafe opened its shop to every coffee lovers.
